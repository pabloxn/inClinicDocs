Clinic Overview

The clinic Worksheet controls the days clinic. This is the space the that the clinician uses to run their clinic from. The days appointments are listed and when clicked upon opens up the details and actions for the clinic.

It is split into two sections:

  1. The clinic appointment list, which displays the list of appointments and their status
  2. The clinic appointment details, which displays the details of the appointment and allows note taking, document uploading and several other features. a case, edit details and various other options.
